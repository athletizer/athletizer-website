Athletizer ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Athletizer mobile application and website at athletizer.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

By registering for or using Athletizer, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to this Privacy Policy. We comply with the Albanian Law No. 9887 "On Protection of Personal Data" and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Definitions

  • Account — A unique account created for you to access our Service.
  • App — The Athletizer mobile application, available on iOS and Android.
  • Device — Any smartphone, tablet, or other hardware used to access our Service.
  • Personal Data — Any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
  • Service — The Athletizer mobile app and website at athletizer.com.
  • Service Provider — Any third-party company that processes data on our behalf.
  • Usage Data — Data collected automatically when you use the Service (e.g., device type, IP address).
  • You / User — The individual accessing or using the Service.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Full name and email address when you create an account
  • Password (stored encrypted — we never see it in plain text)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Bio, playing position, country, and skill level (optional profile fields)
  • Messages sent to other users within the app
  • Information submitted via support or feedback requests

Usage Data (collected automatically)

  • Device type, model, and operating system version
  • IP address and approximate location derived from it
  • App screens visited and time spent
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data
  • Timestamps of interactions (bookings, messages, logins)

Location Data

  • Your real-time location (GPS), used to show pickup games near you
  • Location is accessed only while using the app and only with your permission
  • We do not store your precise GPS coordinates on our servers
  • You can disable location access at any time in your device settings, though some features will be unavailable

Payment Information

  • Game entry fee payments are processed by PayPal
  • We do not store your card or bank details — all payment data is handled directly by PayPal
  • We store a record of the transaction amount, PayPal order ID, and booking status

Push Notification Token

  • A device token used to deliver push notifications about your games, bookings, and messages
  • Collected only if you grant notification permission on your device

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account (contractual basis)
  • To show you pickup soccer games near your location (contractual basis)
  • To process game bookings and payments (contractual basis)
  • To send transactional emails — booking confirmations, cancellations, password resets (contractual basis)
  • To send push notifications about your games, messages, and activity (contractual basis)
  • To enable in-app messaging between players (contractual basis)
  • To process refunds when a game is cancelled (contractual basis)
  • To improve the app, fix bugs, and analyze usage trends (legitimate interest)
  • To prevent fraud or unauthorized use of the Service (legitimate interest)
  • To respond to your support or feedback requests (legitimate interest)
  • To send updates about new features or games in your area, where you have opted in (consent)

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing purposes.

Service Providers

We share data with trusted third-party providers that help us operate the Service. These providers are contractually prohibited from using your data for any purpose other than providing services to us:

  • Supabase — database hosting and user authentication
  • PayPal — payment processing (PayPal Privacy Policy)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Expo — push notification infrastructure

Other Players

When you join a game, your name, profile photo, and skill level are visible to other players in that game. Your email and payment details are never shared with other users.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Athletizer or our users.

With Your Consent

We will not share your data for any other purpose without notifying you and obtaining your explicit consent.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS (HTTPS) and encrypted at rest.

Access to your data is restricted through Row Level Security (RLS) policies — each user can only access their own data unless they have explicit permissions granted by the system.

We restrict internal access to personal data to authorized personnel only, and only where necessary to operate the Service.

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your data to the best of our ability.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the Service. If you request account deletion, your personal data is removed from our systems within 30 days.

We may retain certain data longer where required by law, for fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or to enforce our agreements. Financial transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years for legal and accounting compliance.

7. International Data Transfers

Athletizer is based in Albania. Your data may be processed and stored in servers located outside your country, including within the European Union and the United States, by our Service Providers (Supabase, PayPal, Resend, Expo).

By using the Service, you consent to the transfer of your data to these jurisdictions. We ensure that any such transfers are conducted with appropriate safeguards in place, consistent with applicable data protection laws.

8. Cookies & Tracking

The Athletizer website (athletizer.com) uses minimal browser storage to support core functionality such as form submissions. We do not use advertising cookies or behavioral tracking on our website.

The Athletizer mobile app does not use cookies. Session management is handled securely through Supabase authentication tokens stored locally on your device.

You may clear local storage or app data at any time through your device or browser settings.

9. Communications & Opt-Out

Transactional Emails

We send emails related to your account and bookings — such as booking confirmations, game cancellations, and password resets. These are required to provide the Service and cannot be opted out of while you have an active account.

Push Notifications

We send push notifications about your games, messages, and activity. You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings (Settings → Notifications → Athletizer).

Marketing Communications

If you opt in to marketing updates, we may send you information about new features or games in your area. You can opt out at any time by contacting us at infoathletizer@gmail.com or by using the unsubscribe link in any marketing email.

10. Your Rights

Under Albanian data protection law and, where applicable, GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your account and associated personal data
  • Restriction — Request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances
  • Portability — Request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — Object to processing based on our legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent — Withdraw consent for any processing based on consent at any time
  • Complaint — Lodge a complaint with the Albanian Information and Data Protection Commissioner (idp.al)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at infoathletizer@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Athletizer is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at infoathletizer@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.
