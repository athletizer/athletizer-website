Features

Everything you need to play more

Built for players who just want to play — not manage schedules.

🗺️

Games Near You

See all upcoming pickup games in your city sorted by distance. Filter by skill level so you're always in the right match.

In-Game Chat

Every game has its own group chat. Coordinate meetup spots, share field directions, and get to know your team before kickoff.

👛

Built-In Wallet

Top up your Athletizer wallet and pay for games instantly. Get refunds automatically if a game doesn't reach minimum players.

Skill-Based Matching

Tag your skill level and find games that match. Beginner, intermediate, or advanced — every game has the right players.

Add friends on Athletizer and see which games they're joining. Never show up to a game alone.
